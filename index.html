Good to Know

Common Questions

Where is M2 Training Club?

We're at 8 Nugent Street, Grafton, Auckland 1023 — just minutes from the Auckland CBD, with parking on site. Get directions →

How much is a gym membership?

Memberships start from $25/week for gym floor access. The Perform membership (full access — gym, classes, HYROX, pool, sauna, ice bath) is $45/week. See all memberships →

Is M2 an official HYROX Training Club?

Yes. M2 is an official HYROX Training Club in Auckland. HYROX programming, expert coaching and race-spec equipment are all included in the Perform membership at no extra charge.

Can I try before I commit?

Yes. Try a 7 day trial or grab a 24 hour pass — no lock-in, no sales pitch. Full access to everything M2 has.

What are your opening hours?

We're open Monday–Friday 5:00am–10:00pm and Saturday–Sunday 7:00am–7:00pm. Call us on 09 558 1408 or email reception@m2club.co.nz.

Do you have parking?

Yes — parking is available on site at 8 Nugent Street, Grafton. We're also a short walk from Grafton train station and accessible by bus from the Auckland CBD.
