🎟 Perform Benefits — All Included with Your Passport

Fitness Passport members at M2 receive the complete Perform membership package — our highest tier. No upgrades needed, no extra cost.

Perform Tier
Full Gym Floor Access

Free weights, barbells, cables, cardio machines and more. Train every day within opening hours — no booking needed for gym floor access.

Unlimited Group Classes

HYROX Threshold, HYROX Engine, HYROX Teams and Strength Club — all included. Book your spot through the timetable and train with our expert coaches.

Ice Bath Access

Cold water immersion at 8–12°C — reduce inflammation, speed recovery and build mental toughness. Unlimited access as a Passport member.

Traditional Sauna

Deep heat therapy for muscle recovery, stress relief and better sleep. Use it post-session or on rest days as part of a full recovery protocol.

Spa Pool

Warm hydrotherapy jets to ease tight muscles and unwind after a hard session. Included in your Passport access at no extra cost.

20m Swimming Pool

Lap swimming for active recovery, cardio or low-impact training. Open daily — a full-length pool in central Auckland, included with your Passport.

How to Use Your Passport at M2

1
STEP 01
Check Eligibility

Log in at portal.fitnesspassport.co.nz to confirm M2 is on your employer's plan and your Passport is active.

2
STEP 02
Get Your Card

Sign up through your employer's Fitness Passport portal. You'll receive a physical card and/or access to the Fitness Passport app.

3
STEP 03
Visit M2

Come to 8 Nugent Street, Grafton. Show your Passport card or app at reception — we'll check you in and set up your profile on first visit.

4
STEP 04
Train & Recover

Full gym access, classes and all recovery facilities are yours. Book group fitness through our class timetable to reserve your spot.
